Asar Dagyara

Welcome to the Treasure Hunt of Takshak 24, organized by the Computer Applications Department at Mar Athanasius College of Engineering, Kothamangalam! This event is designed to foster teamwork, sharpen problem-solving abilities, and ignite a passion for technology

GuideLines

Please keep the following in mind:
-Practice good sportsmanship throughout the challenge.
-Prioritize safety at all times.
-Reach out to the organizers if you require any assistance.
-Show respect for the environment by not littering or causing damage to property.

Rules

Team Composition: Each team must consist of exactly 4 enthusiastic members from the same college. Gather your best problem solvers and creative thinkers!
Unique Identity: Register your team with a unique and catchy name, and make sure to appoint a fearless team leader during registration.
Solo Participation: Remember, no participant can join more than one team. Choose your teammates wisely!
College Cap: Each college can have a maximum of three teams competing. Represent your college with pride!
Identification: Participants must carry their college ID at all times during the event. No ID means no participation!
Sequential Solving: Clues must be solved in the given order. Skipping ahead is not allowed! Be ready, as eliminations will take place in each round.
No Extra Time: The event will start on schedule, and organizers won't be responsible for overlaps with other events. Stay focused and be punctual!
Volunteer Decisions: The decisions made by the volunteers will be final and binding.
No Refunds: Once registered, there will be no refunds. Make sure you're ready for the challenge before committing!

Code of Conduct

- Tampering with clues or checkpoints: Play fair; tampering with clues or checkpoints is strictly prohibited.
- Damaging property: Respect the venue and its surroundings. Any damage to property will not be tolerated.
- Using external help: Rely solely on your team's skills; external assistance is not allowed.
- Misbehaving or cheating: Maintain respect and integrity; any form of misconduct or cheating will lead to disqualification.
- Prohibited substances: Stay focused; using prohibited substances is not allowed during the event.

The event will run for approximately 5 to 6 hours. All participants are expected to be punctual, as the event will begin as scheduled. Any rule changes or updates will be communicated before the event starts. The decisions made by the event coordinators are final. Winners will be awarded for 1st and 2nd place, while all teams will receive participation certificates.

We wish you the best of luck—enjoy the adventure and make the most of this exciting experience!
