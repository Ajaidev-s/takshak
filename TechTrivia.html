Tech Trivia

Welcome to the Quiz Challenge of Takshak 24, hosted by the Computer Applications Department at Mar Athanasius College of Engineering, Kothamangalam! This event is crafted to test your knowledge, boost critical thinking, and fuel a love for learning.

GuideLines

Please follow these rules throughout the quiz:
- Fair Play: Maintain integrity and avoid cheating.
- Focus on Learning: Use this opportunity to expand your knowledge.
- Ask for Help: Don’t hesitate to contact the organizers if you need clarification or assistance.
- Respect: Treat the quizmasters, competitors, and environment with respect.

Rules

Team Composition: Each team must consist of exactly 2 members from the same college.
Unique Identity: Register your team with a unique and catchy name, and make sure to appoint a fearless team leader during registration.
Solo Participation: Remember, no participant can join more than one team. Choose your teammates wisely!
Identification: Participants must carry their college ID.
No Extra Time: The event will start on schedule, and organizers won't be responsible for overlaps with other events. Stay focused and be punctual!
Volunteer Decisions: The decisions made by the volunteers will be final and binding.
No Refunds: Once registered, there will be no refunds. Make sure you're ready for the challenge before committing!

Code of Conduct

- Respect for venue and equipment: Take care of the quiz hall and any equipment provided. Damaging property will result in penalties.
- Using unauthorized resources: Rely on your own knowledge and teamwork; the use of unauthorized devices or resources is not allowed.
- Misconduct or cheating: Show respect and honesty. Any form of cheating, disruptive behavior, or disrespect toward others will lead to immediate disqualification.
- Prohibited items: Keep the environment professional; using prohibited items like phones, calculators (if not allowed), or any unfair aids during the quiz is forbidden.

All participants are expected to be punctual, as the event will begin as scheduled. Any rule changes or updates will be communicated before the event starts. The decisions made by the event coordinators are final. Winners will be awarded for 1st and 2nd place, while all teams will receive participation certificates.

Good luck, and enjoy this intellectual journey! We hope it’s both challenging and fun!
