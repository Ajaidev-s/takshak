Valorant Vortex

The tournament will consist of custom 5v5 matches, with a maximum of 8 teams participating. The format will adapt based on the number of registered teams, following a single-elimination knockout structure, progressing through quarterfinals, semifinals, and culminating in a final match to determine the champion.

Equipment Rules

Players will be provided with laptops, keyboards, and mice.
Personal PCs are not allowed.
Players may bring their own keyboard, mouse, and earpieces if preferred.
All players will compete using basic accounts provided by the organizers.

Match Updates

The date and time of matches will be communicated via email and WhatsApp, using the contact information provided during registration.

Wishing the best of luck to all participants. Compete with determination, showcase your teamwork, and enjoy the experience!
