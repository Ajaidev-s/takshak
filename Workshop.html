    • The MCA Department of MACE, Kothamangalam, is hosting a workshop as part of the Takshak event on 25th September 2024, from 9 AM to 12 PM. The workshop will be presented by Mr. Jabir Ismail, a seasoned Principal Software Engineer at Conga and the Founder of Bridgeon. With extensive experience in software engineering and entrepreneurship, Mr. Jabir will share insights on the topic of "Next Gen Application Development." The session will cover emerging trends such as cloud computing, artificial intelligence integration, progressive web apps, and microservices architecture, all of which are shaping the future of modern application development.

    Participants will have the unique opportunity to explore cutting-edge technologies that enable the creation of scalable, efficient, and innovative software solutions suited to the ever-evolving demands of the tech industry. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned developer, this workshop promises to equip attendees with the knowledge and skills to stay ahead in the fast-paced world of software development. A certificate of participation will be awarded to all attendees, making this not only an informative but also a credential-boosting experience.
