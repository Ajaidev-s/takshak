Future Frames

Step into the world of digital artistry with Future Frames, an exciting individual competition where you can express futuristic visions through the power of Microsoft Paint. Whether you're a seasoned artist or just starting out, this event challenges your creativity and precision to craft digital masterpieces.

Event Details

Platform: Microsoft Paint (Provided during the event)
Participation: Individual

Rules and Regulations

1. Participation:
Individual Event: This competition is for individual participants only.
Eligibility: Open to all students with an interest in digital art.
No Changes: Once the event begins, no participant substitutions or changes are allowed.
2. Theme and Content:
Theme: The futuristic theme will be revealed at the start of the competition.
Adherence to Theme: Participants must create artwork based on the provided theme. Off-theme submissions will be disqualified.
Originality: All content must be original. Any plagiarism or use of unlicensed material will lead to disqualification.
3. Tools and Editing:
Design Tool: Only Microsoft Paint is allowed for creating the digital art.
External Assistance: Use of external tools, templates, or third-party assistance is strictly prohibited.
Special Effects: Participants are encouraged to use creative techniques within Paint but should avoid overly complex edits that go beyond the scope of the tool.
4. Submission Guidelines:
Late Submissions: Artwork submitted after the deadline will not be considered unless prior approval is granted due to technical issues.
5. Judging Criteria:
Creativity and Originality: How unique and imaginative the artwork is.
Theme Interpretation: How well the artwork aligns with and represents the futuristic theme.
Aesthetic Appeal: The overall visual impact and design quality.
6. Disqualification:
Malpractice: Any form of plagiarism, cheating, or rule-breaking will result in immediate disqualification.
External Tools: Use of software other than Microsoft Paint is strictly prohibited and will lead to disqualification.
Behavior: Participants engaging in disruptive behavior or violating the event’s code of conduct may be disqualified.
7. Final Decision:
The decisions of the judging panel are final and binding. No appeals or objections will be entertained.

We wish you the best of luck—enjoy the adventure and make the most of this exciting experience!
